# Down To Hell

2D action platformer inspired by Cave Story and Castlevania: Symphony of the Night.

## Premise

You are a miner named James. Broke, and late on rent, you go to work to make some change for your greedy landlord. Toward the bottom of your assigned cave, you see a HUGE daimond. Wanting to pocket it for yourself, you run down there and use your pickaxe to extract it from the hard stone. it comes loose... but so does the bottom of the cave, and you fall. Welcome to hell.

## Gameplay

- movement

The movement prioritizes fluidity and pacing due to the maps large size. Basic run and jump dynamic, along with moves like a double jump and a dash acquired down the line.

- combat

James is armed with only his pickaxe, which he throws like a boomarang. the range, speed, and cool down depend on his level.

- exploration

The world is largely grid/cell based, being very open ended but still having general areas and landmarks.


## Art Style

This game is very inspired by atari and early ms-dos games, with most sprites and background layers only having one color(although we will take creative liberty on this!!)
